# About me

I'm a postgraduate student at Cardiff University, studying for my PhD in Astrophysics with the UKRI Centre for Doctoral Training in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Advanced Computing (AIMLAC). My research concerns cosmological simulations of massive galaxies and their circumgalactic medium. I am supervised by [Dr Freeke van de Voort](https://profiles.cardiff.ac.uk/staff/vandevoortf) of Cardiff University.
