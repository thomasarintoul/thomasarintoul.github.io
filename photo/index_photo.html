Welcome to my website!

Online I'm "That Scottish Creative" - mostly that means I'm a photographer with too many hobbies!

Along with that, I have no single focus when it comes to my photography. I dabble in landscapes and portraits, sports and architecture. Even some product photography!

Feel free to browse my gallery below, and get in touch if you'd like to work together!

Landscapes

"My heart's in the Highlands, my heart is not here,
My heart's in the Highlands, a-chasing the deer." - Robert "Rabbie" Burns

While my landscapes aren't exclusively of the Scottish Highlands, I do love a rugged landscape with towering mountains, deep lochs and dramatic (if somewhat foreboding) skies.

Sports

On your marks...

Sport, I think, brings out the best in people and it shows in sports photography. Whether it's the concentration before a gymnast starts a routine, or the intensity of a swimmer powering through the water, I love capturing those fleeting moments!

  • Learn More

Portraits

"Your pitch doesn't have to be perfect, just credible." - Deborah Meaden

Some might call portraits, and particularly headshot-style portraiture the "boring" photography, but in so many ways, it's your first impression. A good headshot lends credibility - better get it right.

  • Learn More

Street and Architecture

"Our reality isn’t about what’s real, it’s about what we pay attention to." - Hank Green

Too often we lose sight of the beauty and majesty of our everyday surroundings. Whether in a city or a small highland village, it's all too easy to get lost. I like to slow down and take in those details.
